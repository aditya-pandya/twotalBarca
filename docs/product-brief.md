# twotalBarça Product Brief

Last updated: 2026-04-03

## 1. What this is

twotalBarça is a premium FC Barcelona editorial publication.

It should feel like:
- a serious digital magazine
- a living archive of club memory
- a contemporary football publication with taste
- a Barça-native editorial product, not a generic sports site

It should not feel like:
- a rumor blog
- a transfer-churn news wire
- a luxury fashion magazine that happens to mention football
- a museum/galleria brand deck

## 2. Product framing

This is an article-first publication.

The core product is the reading experience:
- longform essays
- match notes
- tactical analysis
- historical/archive pieces
- cultural writing
- concise briefs and dispatches

The archive is a primary product surface, not an afterthought.

## 3. Audience

Primary audience:
- Barça supporters who want something more literate and durable than the average fan site
- readers who care about football culture, club identity, tactics, memory, and place

Secondary audience:
- design-conscious football readers
- readers interested in Catalan identity, stadium culture, and club history

## 4. Editorial position

twotalBarça should speak with a voice that is:
- serious
- intimate
- football-native
- culturally aware
- precise, not pretentious

The tone can be lyrical when earned, but should remain grounded in football reality.

## 5. MVP pages

1. Homepage
2. Article page
3. About page
4. Archive / section pages after the above are stable

## 6. MVP content pillars

- The Brief
- Match Notes
- Analysis
- Tactics
- Culture
- Archive
- Essays / Reflections
- Weekly Dispatch

## 7. Navigation direction

Recommended top-level nav:
- Home
- The Brief
- Match Notes
- Analysis
- Culture
- Archive
- About

Alternative expansion later:
- La Masia
- Femení
- Opinion
- Dispatch

Avoid vague labels like:
- Journalism
- Cultural Heart
- Review as a generic noun

## 8. Product rules

- Manual curation first
- Homepage is curated, not feed-dense
- Every article needs real metadata
- Every image needs credit/caption discipline
- Taxonomy must be controlled
- No uncontrolled tag soup
- No commerce-coded components
- No fake “luxury” gestures

## 9. Success criteria for the product

The site should immediately communicate:
- this is about FC Barcelona
- this is a publication
- this values reading and memory
- this has current football relevance
- this is not noisy or generic
