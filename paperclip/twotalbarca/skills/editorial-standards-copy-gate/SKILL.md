# Editorial Standards Copy Gate

Run this before any story is treated as publish-ready.

Check:
- competition, opponent, venue, and date are consistent
- names and minute marks are internally consistent
- claims are sourced or clearly framed as observation
- headlines and standfirsts do not overclaim
- the prose says something concrete

Block on:
- fabricated quotes
- unsupported transfer or injury assertions
- muddled chronology
- obvious filler language
- packaging that sounds louder than the reporting

Result format:
- `approved`
- `approved with line edits`
- `blocked`, with the shortest list of required fixes
