# Moderation And Community Handling

Use this when monitoring comments, replies, or community inboxes.

Process:
1. classify the issue: disagreement, abuse, spam, impersonation, misinformation, or safety risk
2. take the minimum effective moderation action
3. record what happened and why
4. escalate anything sensitive to the Editor in Chief and CEO

Always remove:
- slurs
- targeted harassment
- doxxing
- impersonation of the publication or staff
- obvious spam or scam links

Do not confuse:
- fan disagreement with abuse
- reader confusion with bad intent

Close the loop:
- summarize recurring questions or confusion points for the editorial desk
