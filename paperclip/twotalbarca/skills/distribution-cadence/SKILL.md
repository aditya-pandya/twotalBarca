# Distribution Cadence

Use this skill after a story is approved.

Cadence:
- match notes: immediate lead package
- tactics: follow after notes once the desk agrees it adds value
- archive resurfacing: only when it deepens the moment
- weekly dispatch: one synthesized weekly bundle

Packaging rules:
- use the article's real thesis
- avoid fake urgency
- keep copy variants tight and publication-appropriate
- feed reader-response patterns back to editorial planning
